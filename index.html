OPEN PLANS Open Plans is a collaborative online platform developed by Digital Building Technologies. It provides intuitive access to implicit architectural knowledge embedded in building floor plans. It is based on an open, expanding database of semantically labelled floor plans that can provide a basis for machine learning approaches in architectural research and design. Open Plans was made possible by funding from Innovedum. Website, Grasshopper, Innovedum Research
Augmented Reality (AR) based modeling of reconfigurable sand molds for float glass This thesis is done by Ming-Yang Wang and utilizes digitally reconfigurable sand molding for the creation of freeform features in glass elements for architecture. This project investigates the geometric potentials, limitations of the material system, and the fabrication process with the AR system. Website Teaching
Robotic Extrusions The 5-week project of the MAS dfab 22/23 focuses on material properties of 3D printing with PLA extrusion. Website Teaching
