SIMON GRIFFIOEN is a researcher in architecture at Digital Building Technologies, ETH Zurich. He graduated from Delft University of Technology in 2018. He continued his studies at ETH Zurich and received his MAS ETH in Digital Fabrication in 2021. His work revolves around the intersection of architecture, digital fabrication, and machine intelligence. Simon is engaged in teaching and researching changes in architectural design and production that result from introducing digital manufacturing techniques. With a background in both architecture and data science, he has a particular interest in combining data and materials.
